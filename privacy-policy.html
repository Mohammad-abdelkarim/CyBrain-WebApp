Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

CyBrain LLC (“CyBrain,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is a US-registered technology company providing software development, AI solutions, cybersecurity consulting, cloud infrastructure, and data analytics services.

By using our website, contacting us, submitting a form, booking a consultation, or communicating with us through email or WhatsApp, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, and message details.
  • Project information you voluntarily share with us, including requirements, business goals, budget expectations, and technical needs.
  • Communication information from emails, WhatsApp messages, consultation bookings, or other business conversations.
  • Technical information such as browser type, device information, approximate location, pages visited, and website usage data.
  • Review-related information if you choose to leave feedback through Trustpilot or another third-party platform.

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • Improve our website, services, communication, and customer experience.
  • Maintain business records and comply with applicable legal, tax, or contractual obligations.
  • Protect our website, systems, clients, and business operations from misuse or security risks.

4. Cookies and Third-Party Tools

Our website may use third-party tools or external services, such as booking links, WhatsApp links, Trustpilot widgets, Google Fonts, analytics tools, hosting providers, or security-related tools. These services may collect limited technical or usage information according to their own privacy policies.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website features.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only when necessary for business operations, such as:

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no online transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Data Retention

We keep personal and business information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including communication, project delivery, business records, legal obligations, dispute resolution, and security purposes.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain uses of your personal information. You may also request that we update or remove information you previously provided, subject to legal, contractual, or legitimate business requirements.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for businesses and professional users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can review and remove it when appropriate.
